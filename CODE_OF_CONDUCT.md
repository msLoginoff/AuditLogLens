# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Participants in this project are expected to keep discussions respectful, focused, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Assume technical disagreement is normal.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the project.
- Respect maintainers' time and review scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or insulting language toward contributors or users.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly pushing unrelated topics into project discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block users who repeatedly violate this code of conduct.

For serious concerns, contact the maintainer privately through GitHub.
